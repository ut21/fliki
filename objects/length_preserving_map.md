# Length-Preserving Sequence Map

For sets $X,Y$, write

$$
f : X^+ \xrightarrow{\mathrm{lp}} Y^+
$$

to denote a function mapping every nonempty sequence over $X$ to a sequence over $Y$ of the same length.

For $x=(x_1,\ldots,x_n)$,

$$
f(x)=(y_1,
\ldots,y_n).
$$
