# Hahn — Theoretical Limitations of Self-Attention in Neural Sequence Models

**Transactions of the Association for Computational Linguistics**, 8:156–171, 2020.

Establishes that softmax-attention transformers with fixed parameters cannot recognize Parity, Majority, or Dyck-2, because output sensitivity to any individual input position vanishes as $n \to \infty$.

## Results

- [Hahn Sensitivity Bound](../results/hahn_sensitivity_bound.md)

## Relation to other work

- [Chiang and Cholak 2022](chiang2022.md): circumvents this bound using hard attention.
- [Pérez et al. 2021](perez2021.md): arbitrary-precision hard attention is Turing complete — the Hahn bound does not apply.
