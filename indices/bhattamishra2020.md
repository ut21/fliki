# Bhattamishra, Ahuja, and Goyal — On the Ability and Limitations of Transformers to Recognize Formal Languages

**Proceedings of EMNLP**, 2020.

Positive results: transformers can simulate any DFA and recognize certain counter languages beyond the regular languages. Also establishes limitations on languages requiring unbounded memory.

## Results

- [Transformer DFA Simulation](../results/transformer_dfa_simulation.md)

## Relation to other work

- [Angluin et al. 2023](angluin2023.md): exact characterization of masked hard-attention transformers as star-free — a strictly weaker model than the general transformer studied here.
- [Merrill et al. 2022](merrill2022.md): saturated transformers are in $\mathbf{TC}^0$, an upper bound complementing the lower bounds here.
